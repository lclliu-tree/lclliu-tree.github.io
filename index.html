Hi, My name is Landon Liu

I am a Ph.D. student in Logic, Computation and Methodology at Carnegie Mellon University in the Department of Philosophy.

On the practical side, I'm interested in research that can improve the lives of Canadians, particularly along the lines of strengthening our democracy as well as poverty alleviation and education access.
